# The Seven Arithmetic Operations

## Addition

term \( + \) term \( = \) sum

## Subtraction

term \( - \) term \( = \) difference

## Multiplication

factor \( \times \) factor \( = \) product

## Division

numerator (täljare) \( / \) denominator (nämnare) \( = \) quotient (kvot)

## Exponentiation (Potensräkning)

\( \text{base}^{\text{exponent}} = \) value (värde)

## Roots (Rotutdragning)

\( \sqrt{\text{radicand}} = \) value

## Logarithms

\( \log_{\text{base (bas)}}(\text{argument}) = \) exponent
